About Me

I'm currently a graduate student at the University of Florida pursuing a Master of Arts in Mass Communication with a specialization in Web Design and Online Communication. As a Web Design Teacher, I'm passionate about sharing knowledge and helping students discover the creative potential of digital design.

My academic and professional journey has given me a strong foundation in web technologies, user experience principles, and design theory. I'm particularly interested in how design can create meaningful digital experiences that connect with users.

Beyond academics and teaching, I believe in the power of continuous learning and hands-on practice. I enjoy working on personal web development projects that challenge me to explore new technologies and design approaches.

My goal is to bridge the gap between beautiful design and functional user experiences, creating digital solutions that are both aesthetically pleasing and highly usable.

My Hobbies & Interests

💻 Website Development

I love working on personal web development projects that allow me to experiment with new technologies and design concepts. Each project teaches me something new about user experience and modern web standards.

🎨 Drawing

I enjoy sketching and studying the techniques of old masters. Drawing helps me understand composition, form, and visual hierarchy - skills that directly translate to my digital design work and enhance my creative problem-solving abilities.

✈️ Traveling

Traveling exposes me to different cultures, design philosophies, and ways of thinking. Each destination provides fresh inspiration for my design work and helps me understand how users from different backgrounds interact with digital experiences.

🎵 Music

I'm passionate about listening to music and learning about its history. Understanding how musical composition creates emotional responses helps me think about how design elements can evoke feelings and create memorable user experiences.

🏋️‍♀️ Weight Lifting

Weight lifting teaches me discipline, goal-setting, and the importance of consistent practice. The mental focus and perseverance required in fitness directly applies to tackling complex design challenges and long-term projects.
